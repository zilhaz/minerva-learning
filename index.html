Minerva Learning is a new, dynamic education program that accelerates learning

The program features a dynamic and personalized path for each student so they can learn at their own pace and style rather than being forced to adjust to a static lesson plan to optimize learning. Studies have shown that this approach can bring an average student all the way to the 98th percentile of performance.

Minerva Learning features four key areas of support for students

Lessons

Lessons delivered to students based on their needs to make sure their time is well spent

Activities

Activities that engage students through the process and help them learn and do so with fun

Problem Sets

Testing proficiency in all areas covered by lessons and activities to measure progress for every student

Dashboard

Identifying strengths and weaknesses of each student to optimize the learning path

Minerva Learning

The struggles of the educational system in the United States are well documented, especially when compared to other nations of the world. Numerous studies, blue ribbon panels, laws and regulations have been pursued, and billions of dollars have been spent on school reforms. Nevertheless, it is safe to say that education has not meaningfully changed in the past quarter century and educational results have not improved.

The dynamic components of the Minerva system are designed around established theories of learning that guide effective teaching. Traits of learning are tracked using an introduction of activities that are tagged with a wide range of skills and learning tendencies. As students move through various activities, the Minerva system is learning about them. Utilizing machine learning to bring a higher level of efficacy to the process makes Minerva unique.
